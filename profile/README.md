# Open Weald Software

Open Weald Software develops open source software that helps communities manage projects, funding, and civic infrastructure. Our primary project is Commons Atlas, a platform designed to support the long-term stewardship of community institutions, shared public systems, and community projects that benefit the public good.

The work in this organization focuses on building practical tools that help communities organize complex projects and sustain the infrastructure they rely on.

---

## Commons Atlas

Commons Atlas is an open source platform for managing civic infrastructure projects and the funding, reporting, and compliance systems that support them.

Communities frequently rely on layered funding sources, multi-year projects, and collaboration between public institutions, nonprofit organizations, and private partners. Commons Atlas is designed to make those systems easier to manage in a clear and structured way.

The platform focuses on:

- Project and program management  
- Grant and funding tracking  
- Budget development and planning  
- Expense allocation, tracking, and reimbursement  
- Compliance and reporting requirements  
- Long-term stewardship of community infrastructure

Commons Atlas is currently the primary focus of development within Open Weald Software.

---

## Design Approach

Software developed within this organization is guided by a few practical principles:

- Durability over novelty  
- Clear and explicit data structures  
- Systems designed for real institutional work  
- Open standards and transparent architecture  
- Long-term maintainability

The goal is to build tools that communities can rely on for decades, not just development cycles.
